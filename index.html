Welcome to Meal-Plan-O, a one stop meal organizer and planner!


~We povide the organizational tools to help you keep track of what you eat during the day,

with the help of separating your days with separate meal sections.


~We also provide you a way to search for a specific type of food and give its macro-nutrient information,

such as its calories, proteins, carbs and fat intake, in hopes to make your life a lot easier!


Now that you know about us, let's get started!


There are three simple steps:

1) Simply choose a day tab up top to start planning your meals!

2) Create a meal section

3) Start adding a food items with the edit icon!
